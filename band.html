About the Band Activity

The Band activity is a vibrant and engaging program that allows students to explore their musical talents, collaborate with peers, and perform at various events. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced musician, there's a place for you in the band!

Details

Our band practices regularly to prepare for school events, competitions, and community performances. Students learn various instruments, music theory, and teamwork skills.
